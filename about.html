About Us

Founded in 2010, GreenLawn Care has been serving the Springfield area with top-quality lawn care and landscaping services for over a decade. What started as a small family business with just one truck and a lawn mower has grown into the area's most trusted lawn care provider.

Our mission is simple: to provide exceptional lawn care services that enhance the beauty and value of our clients' properties while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and customer service.

Our Team

Meet the GreenLawn Care Team

Our team consists of certified horticulturists, experienced landscapers, and dedicated lawn care professionals who take pride in their work. We invest in ongoing training to stay current with the latest lawn care techniques and environmental best practices.
